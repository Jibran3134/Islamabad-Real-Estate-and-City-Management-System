Our History & Legacy

Established in 2026, the Islamabad Real Estate Management System (IRMS) was formed to bring unprecedented transparency and digital convenience to the real estate sector of the capital.

Before our inception, property trading lacked centralized monitoring. Today, we oversee thousands of legitimate properties, ensuring that every buyer, agent, and seller operates securely under the city's legal framework.
